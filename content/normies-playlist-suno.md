# Normies Playlist — Suno Song Briefs

Ten tracks for the upcoming **Normies Playlist** hub experience. Each song is rooted in **official Normies lore** — not hub games — drawn from [api.normies.art](https://api.normies.art), [hackathon.normies.art](https://hackathon.normies.art), and the on-chain collection by **Serc & Yigit**.

**Official pillars to reference**
- 10,000 on-chain generative faces (token IDs `0`–`9999`)
- 40×40 monochrome bitmaps — pixel on `#48494b`, background `#e3e5e4`
- 8 trait categories: Type, Gender, Age, Hair Style, Facial Feature, Eyes, Expression, Accessory
- Four Types: **Human**, **Cat**, **Alien**, **Agent**
- Canvas metadata: **Level**, **Action Points**, **Pixel Count**, **Customized**
- **NormiesCanvas** — XOR transform layer, flip pixels, version history
- **Burn to transform** — sacrifice Normies to reshape another; supply shrinks; burned art persists on-chain
- **ERC-8004 agents** — live personas from traits + canvas state
- Taglines: *"On-Chain Generative Faces"*, *"Build With 10,000 On-Chain Faces"*, *"Prove You're a Normie"*

**How to use in Suno**
1. Paste **Style** into the style / description field.
2. Paste **Lyrics** into the lyrics field.
3. Export as `01-track-name.mp3` … `10-track-name.mp3`.
4. When all 10 are ready, we'll build the playlist in the hub.
